Who Am I?

Hello, my name is Derek Smith and during high schooI I spent 3 years in the lawn and landscaping industry. While I worked there, I acquired many useful skills such as hard work, eye for detail, and having excellent communication skills while working on a team. However, my true enjoyment and passion for as long as I can remember was for technology and every aspect of it. During my Senior year of high school, I was enrolled in the Summit Technology Academy, which is a career preparation school that I attended for Software Development. Summit Tech motivated me more to pursue a career in the IT industry, as I figured out that I loved coding. This led me to now attending Centriq IT Training to hopefully gain a wide variety of base knowledge for modern websites. With me gaining experience daily, I am determined to keep expanding my knowledge in the Software industry, and when you have any potential position openings, I would love to speak with you further about them.
